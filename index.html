Welcome to Dallas Brethren Assembly

Hello, Warm greetings to you in the name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. We are so glad that you are here.Our goal is to follow the pattern of the early New Testament church as recorded in the Bible. We have the observance of the Lord’s Supper instituted by the Lord, Sunday school, and word ministry every week. We as a local church stand for the edification of the believers, and gospel proclamation.You are invited to visit us and consider becoming a member here where you can grow spiritually. No matter what, remember this truth -God loves you and He has a wonderful and beautiful plan for your life. DBA is a church that wants to communicate God’s loving plan & purpose for your life.

Our Journey: A Legacy of Faith and Fellowship

Dallas Brethren Assembly began with a heartfelt desire to establish a community of believers dedicated to the simple, yet profound, principles of New Testament Christianity. From our humble beginnings in [Year of Founding], we have been steadfast in our commitment to biblical teaching, the fellowship of believers, the breaking of bread, and prayer. Over the decades, we have seen countless lives touched by the grace of God and have been blessed to witness families grow in faith within our walls. Our history is a testament to God's faithfulness and the enduring power of His Word. We believe in the priesthood of all believers, valuing every member's contribution to the body of Christ. Our gatherings are characterized by a spirit of mutual edification and a focus on Christ as the head of the church. We strive to be a beacon of light in Dallas, sharing the hope of the Gospel through our lives and our words. Our mission is to glorify God by making disciples who love God, love each other, and love the world. We are called to teach the whole counsel of God, to worship Him in spirit and truth, and to serve our community with compassion and integrity. We envision a church where every individual is equipped to live a life that honors God and makes a lasting impact for His kingdom.

Our Worship Gatherings

Our weekly gatherings are the heartbeat of our community, a time when we come together as one body to worship God and be nourished by His Word. We invite you to experience the warmth of our fellowship and the depth of our biblical teaching.

🌅

Sunday Morning Worship

Our primary service begins promptly at 10:00 AM. This gathering includes a time of open worship and prayer, a reflection on the Lord's Supper, and a practical, expository message from the Bible. We conclude around 11:30 AM, followed by a time of fellowship.

🍞

The Lord's Supper

Central to our worship, the Lord's Supper is observed weekly, reminding us of Christ's sacrifice and resurrection. All who believe in Jesus Christ as their Lord and Savior are welcome to participate.

📖

Bible Teaching Focus

We are committed to the systematic teaching of the Bible, verse by verse and book by book, to provide a deeper understanding of God's truth and its application to our daily lives.

☕

Fellowship

Following our service, we encourage everyone to stay for coffee and light refreshments, providing an excellent opportunity to connect with one another and build lasting relationships.

📍

Location & Parking

Dallas Brethren Assembly is located at 123 Christian Way, Dallas, TX 75201. We offer ample free parking on-site. Our facility is easily accessible, and we have dedicated spaces for visitors.

A Special Welcome for Our Guests

We understand that visiting a new church can sometimes feel a little overwhelming. Our desire is for you to feel comfortable and welcomed from the moment you arrive. Expect a warm greeting, a friendly atmosphere, and a service focused on the worship of God and the teaching of His Word. There's no dress code, just come as you are. We have age-appropriate ministries for children during the service, and our ushers are always available to answer any questions you may have. We can't wait to meet you!
